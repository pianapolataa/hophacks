Welcome to MedTwin

Your personalized digital twin for safer, faster, and more effective hypertension treatment.

What is MedTwin?

MedTwin simplifies the process of finding the right medication dosage by creating a digital twin of the patient, giving patients and doctors a safe way to experiment and predict responses before trying them in real life.

Why It Matters

Simplifies trial-and-error process by creating a digital twin powered by clinical trial data and machine learning. The digital twin can learn a patient’s unique sensitivity to medication, and guide doctors and patients toward the right dosage and timing more quickly and confidently.

How It Works

1. Input the baseline data (blood pressure, heart rate, age, BMI, lifestyle).
2. Run simulations to see 8-hour predictions.
3. Get optimized dosage and timing suggestions.

Features

- Data input page for personal details.
- Interactive experimentation dashboard.
- Side effect monitoring and alerts.
- Suggestions on when and how to take Lisinopril.
